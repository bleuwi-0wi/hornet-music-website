About HORNET MUSIC

HORNET MUSIC is a feature-packed Discord bot designed to bring high-quality music to your server. Whether you're chilling with friends or hosting a party, HORNET MUSIC has you covered with seamless playback, interactive controls, and more!

Features

  • Play music from YouTube with ease
  • Interactive button controls for playback
  • Queue management: move, remove, and skip songs
  • Volume control at your fingertips
  • Lyrics support powered by Genius

Commands

  • !j - Join your voice channel
  • !p <song name/url> - Play a song
  • !controls - Open the control panel
  • !lyrics - Show lyrics for the current song
  • !queue - View the current queue
  • !move <from> <to> - Move a song in the queue
  • !remove <index> - Remove a song from the queue
  • !sk - Skip the current song
  • !l - Leave the voice channel
